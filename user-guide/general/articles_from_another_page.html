Display articles from another page

One page displays usually its linked articles. But it is also possible to display the articles from another page, even this another page isn't online or visible in the navigation menu.

This is done through the <ion:articles /> tag.

<ion:articles from="page_url_one, page_url_two">
    <ion:article />		
</ion:articles>
